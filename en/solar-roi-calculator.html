Understanding Solar Return on Investment (ROI)

Investing in solar panels is one of the best financial decisions for a homeowner, largely due to the 30% Federal Solar Tax Credit (ITC) in the United States, which drastically reduces the net cost of the system.

Net Cost: $$ \text{Net Cost} = \text{Total Cost} - (\text{Total Cost} \times \text{Tax Credit \%}) $$
Payback Period (Years): $$ \text{Years} = \frac{\text{Net Cost}}{\text{Monthly Savings} \times 12} $$

Once the payback period is over, all electricity generated by the system is pure profit for the remaining life of the panels (typically 25+ years).
