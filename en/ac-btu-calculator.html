ENERGY STAR® AC Sizing Guidelines

Buying an air conditioner that is too large will cool the room quickly but fail to remove humidity, leaving the room feeling damp. Buying one too small will run constantly and drive up your energy bill.

The standard rule of thumb is that you need about 20 BTUs per square foot of living space. You must also add 4,000 BTUs if the unit is for a kitchen, and adjust by 10% up or down depending on sun exposure.
