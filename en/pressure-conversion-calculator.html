Global Pressure Standards

In the engineering world, different regions and industries rely on different units for measuring fluid and gas pressure:

  • PSI (Pounds per Square Inch): The standard in the USA for pneumatics, hydraulics, and tire pressure.
  • Bar & kPa: The European and international metric standard. 1 Bar is roughly equal to atmospheric pressure at sea level.
  • inH2O (Inches of Water Column): Extensively used in HVAC systems to measure very low static air pressures inside ductwork.
