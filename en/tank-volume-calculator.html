Standard Tank Volume Formulas

In industrial fluid storage, determining the exact volume of a vessel requires calculating its geometric cubic volume first, and then converting it into liquid units like US Gallons or Oil Barrels (bbl).

Vertical Cylinder Volume: $$ V = \pi \times r^2 \times h $$ Rectangular Tank Volume: $$ V = l \times w \times h $$

Essential Conversions used in the USA

  • 1 Cubic Foot ($ft^3$) = 7.48052 US Gallons.
  • 1 Oil Barrel (bbl) = 42 US Gallons.
  • 1 Cubic Meter ($m^3$) = 264.172 US Gallons.

Our calculator automatically applies the 7.48052 multiplier to ensure exact US Gallon outputs for petrochemical and agricultural storage systems.
