ASHRAE Ventilation Standards

Air Changes per Hour (ACH) represents how many times the total volume of air within a space is completely replaced in one hour. Proper ACH is mandated by ASHRAE standards for indoor air quality and infection control.

Calculate ACH from CFM: $$ ACH = \frac{CFM \times 60}{\text{Room Volume } (ft^3)} $$
Calculate required CFM: $$ CFM = \frac{\text{Room Volume } (ft^3) \times ACH}{60} $$

Common ACH Guidelines

  • Offices & Classrooms: 3 to 6 ACH
  • Commercial Kitchens: 15 to 20 ACH
  • Hospital Operating Rooms: 20+ ACH
