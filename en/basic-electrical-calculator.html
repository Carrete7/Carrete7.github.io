Understanding Ohm's Law (DC)

Ohm's Law states that the current through a conductor between two points is directly proportional to the voltage across the two points. It is the foundation of all electrical engineering.

Voltage Formula: $$ V = I \times R $$ Power Formula: $$ P = V \times I $$
